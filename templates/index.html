How Heart Analysis Works

Humara AI model **Ejection Fraction** (dil kitna blood pump kar raha hai) aur **Serum Creatinine** (kidney function jo heart stress batata hai) ko analyze karta hai.

  • • Normal EF: 55% - 70%
  • • Normal Creatinine: 0.7 - 1.3 mg/dL
🧠

Stroke Risk Assessment

Neurological stroke ka khatra **Hypertension** aur **Glucose levels** se juda hota hai. AI aapke BMI aur blood sugar markers ko scan karke vascular blockage ka risk nikalta hai.

  • • Target Glucose: < 140 mg/dL
  • • Ideal BMI: 18.5 - 24.9
